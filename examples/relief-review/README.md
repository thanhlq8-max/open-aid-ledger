5. Keep all real evidence and private contact details outside the public repository.

## What these samples demonstrate

- Pseudonymous beneficiary code usage.
- Public-safe need summary.
- Partner confirmation without personal exposure.
- Decision record that does not imply donation activation.
- Future ledger mapping without private details.

## What these samples do not demonstrate

- Real campaign activation.
- Real donation collection.
- Real wallet publication.
- Real transfer or custody workflow.
- Legal, tax, charity-registration, or financial advice.
