# Governance

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- policy changes;
- wallet metadata review;
- ledger reconciliation;
- privacy review;
- campaign approval;
- report publication;
- incident response.

## Decision model

For non-financial documentation changes, one maintainer review is acceptable.

For wallet changes, campaign activation, disbursement records, or privacy-sensitive changes, use at least two maintainer reviews where possible.

## Conflict of interest

A maintainer must disclose a conflict if a campaign or disbursement benefits:

- themselves;
- relatives;
- close friends;
- business partners;
- projects they control;
- private groups they administer.

Conflict disclosures should be public unless doing so would expose beneficiary private data.

## No unilateral custody at scale

Single-maintainer wallets are allowed only for small pilots and must be disclosed. Larger campaigns should move to multisig.
