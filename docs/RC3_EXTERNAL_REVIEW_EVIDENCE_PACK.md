## Purpose

This document defines the evidence pack required before any later activation proposal can be considered.

RC3 does not activate donations, publish real wallet addresses, add custody automation, or authorize transfer automation.

## Evidence pack contents

The evidence pack should collect:

1. external reviewer identities or review roles;
2. review scope and dates;
3. reviewed documents;
4. unresolved findings;
5. accepted risk items;
6. activation blockers;
7. final recommendation;
8. maintainer response.

## Required review areas

External review must cover:

- wallet publication process;
- wallet-change procedure;
- campaign lifecycle process;
- beneficiary privacy controls;
- public status page wording;
- ledger validation;
- public safety scanner behavior;
- emergency freeze procedure;
- conflict-of-interest handling;
- donation activation gate.

## Activation rule

A future activation proposal is blocked unless the evidence pack shows:

- all high-severity findings resolved;
- beneficiary privacy controls reviewed;
- wallet publication precheck completed;
- public status page reviewed;
- donation activation checklist reviewed;
- maintainer signoff recorded.

## Non-activation statement

RC3 is an evidence-pack checkpoint only. It does not approve donation collection.
