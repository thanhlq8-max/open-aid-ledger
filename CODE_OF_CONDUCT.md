# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful.
- Protect vulnerable people.
- Do not shame beneficiaries.
- Do not pressure anyone to donate.
- Do not harass maintainers or recipients.
- Do not publish private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- doxxing;
- threats;
- hate speech;
- donation coercion;
- scam promotion;
- fake charity claims;
- posting seed phrases/private keys;
- pressuring beneficiaries to reveal identity;
- using hardship cases for marketing without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, block users, or report abuse when safety or privacy is at risk.
