# Campaign: 2026 Community Hardship Support

## Campaign ID

```text
COMMUNITY-HARDSHIP-2026
```

## Purpose

Support children, families, homeless people, and vulnerable community members facing urgent hardship.

## Allowed use

- food;
- medicine;
- school supplies;
- temporary shelter support;
- emergency family support;
- basic work tools;
- transport for urgent needs.

## Not allowed

- trading capital;
- loan repayment for speculation;
- gambling;
- political funding;
- profit-seeking investment;
- non-transparent cash handoff without record.

## Beneficiary privacy

Use beneficiary codes. Do not publish raw identity documents.

## Reporting

Every disbursement must be recorded in `ledger/disbursements.csv`.
