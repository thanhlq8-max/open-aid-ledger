# Campaign: 2026 Trader Community Giving

## Campaign ID

```text
TRADER-GIVING-2026
```

## Purpose

Give traders and market participants a transparent way to donate a part of their legitimate profits to social support.

This campaign is not for funding trading activity.

## Allowed use

- hardship support for community members;
- children and family support;
- homeless/vulnerable support;
- education and basic tools;
- emergency living costs.

## Not allowed

- margin call rescue;
- funding a trading account;
- prop challenge fees;
- copy trading;
- investment pooling;
- trading signal access;
- PnL sharing;
- refund promises.

## Trader-facing message

Profitable traders may choose to contribute voluntarily. Small individual contributions may be weak alone; pooled transparent giving can support larger real-world impact.

## Required transparency

All incoming and outgoing transfers should be logged.
